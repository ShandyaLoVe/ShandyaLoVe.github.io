About Me

Hello! I'm Shandya (Lǐ Línshān), a master's student at Zhejiang University specializing in Smart Sensing and Machine Intelligence.

My research focuses on Information Fusion, Computer Vision, and Embodied AI, bridging theory and practical applications to build intelligent systems for real-world challenges.

Currently, I am a member of the IVLAB. My supervisor is Professor Huiliang Shen. Runmin Zhang is my senior mentor, and Siheng Wang is my long-term collaborator.
